Verve Promo Project

Client Verve
Tools Adobe After Effects, Blender
Expertise Motion Design & 3D Asset Creation

This project combined dynamic motion design with detailed 3D asset creation to deliver a visually compelling promo. We used Blender to model and animate 3D assets, providing depth and realism to the visuals.

These assets were then integrated into a motion design workflow in Adobe After Effects, where compositing, animation, and effects were applied to bring the concept to life. The blend of 3D elements with motion graphics allowed us to create an engaging and polished promo that effectively represents the Verve brand.
