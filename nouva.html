Nouva Scarf

Tool: Cinema4D, Adobe After Effects Client: Nouva

Creative Approach

For Nouva, a fashion brand known for its minimalist yet elegant aesthetics, I was tasked with creating a product-focused motion visual that highlighted the flow, texture, and elegance of their signature scarves.

The goal was to bring the lightweight, graceful quality of the Nouva Scarf to life through motion blending realism with artistic direction. Rather than showing the product in a conventional way, the idea was to make it dance on screen, reflecting movement, airiness, and luxury.
