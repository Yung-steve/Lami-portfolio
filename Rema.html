Rema Cam Motion Visualizer O2 Arena 2025

Client: Rema / Tropical

Illustrator: Wallstreet (Abduldabar)

Tool Used: Adobe After Effects


For Rema’s 2025 O2 Arena concert, I was tasked with animating a "Kiss Cam"-style visualizer a fun, interactive moment designed to amplify crowd engagement during the live show. The goal was to blend Afro-futurism aesthetics with concert-ready energy playful, bold, and in sync with Rema's sound and on-stage vibe.

The concept needed to feel organic to the performance while standing out as a memorable moment for fans. Using After Effects, I animated a looping visual that mimicked the classic kiss cam frame but reimagined it with vibrant, Rema-style twists.
