Flavour Lab Brand Animation / Short Rollout

Tool: Procreate Dream, After Effects

Type: Brand Animation / Short Rollout

Illustration: 7ife (Tife)

Creative Direction: Niyi Okeowo


Flavour Lab is a bold, modern restaurant concept that blends experimental cuisine with vibrant visual culture. I was brought on to create a short animated rollout to support their brand launch something eye-catching, stylish, and full of flavor.

The goal was to introduce Flavour Lab with a visual identity that feels as fresh and creative as their food. The rollout animation needed to express their experimental vibe a mix of culinary science, street flavor, and urban design.
