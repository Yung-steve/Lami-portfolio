Character animation is where illustration meets personality. For these projects, my goal was to bring still designs to life , giving characters expression, timing, and movement that connects with viewers on an emotional or entertaining level.

Each animation begins with a simple concept: “How would this character move, react, and feel in the world they live in?” Whether it’s a mascot for a brand, a short animated loop for social media, or part of a narrative sequence, I focus on making the motion feel natural, playful, and engaging.
